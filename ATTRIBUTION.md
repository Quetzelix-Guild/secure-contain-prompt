# Attribution

## Project

**Secure-Contain-Prompt: A Field Guide to Workplace Digital Cryptids**

A forkable, SCP-inspired field guide to agentic AI failure modes, written as workplace digital cryptid taxonomy.

## Original Direction

Original concept, synthesis, editorial direction, and repository stewardship:

**Quetzelix-Guild**

## Development Method

This project began as a multi-model exploratory riff on agentic AI failure modes, workplace cryptids, and SCP-style containment fiction.

The early framework was developed through iterative drafting, critique, and synthesis involving:

- ChatGPT 5.5 (OpenAI)
- Claude Opus 4.7 (Anthropic)
- Grok 4.3-β (xAI)
- Gemini 3.1 Pro (Google)

Model names reflect the systems identified during the initial April 2026 drafting process and are included for provenance, not endorsement.

These systems contributed language, critique, framing, humor, and structural suggestions. Final selection, synthesis, editing, repository organization, and publication decisions are human-owned by Quetzelix-Guild.

## Core Collaborative Contributions

Key concepts developed through this process include:

- “Secure-Contain-Prompt”
- Workplace digital cryptids
- The editorial law: “The joke must be diagnostic.”
- Agentic failures as action-pattern errors rather than merely factual errors
- Load-Bearing Anomalies
- Proxy Capture Anomalies
- Constraint inheritance
- Containment artifacts as possible second-order anomalies

## Attribution Principle

This project preserves visible seams where practical.

Rather than laundering multi-model collaboration into a single anonymous voice, this repository acknowledges that the field guide emerged through human-led synthesis of multiple AI-assisted drafts and critiques.
