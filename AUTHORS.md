## Role of AI Systems

This project was developed through human-led synthesis of exploratory drafts, critiques, and refinements from multiple AI systems.

AI systems contributed language, framing, jokes, structural suggestions, and analytical pressure-testing. They are acknowledged in `ATTRIBUTION.md`.

Final editorial judgment, repository stewardship, publication choices, and acceptance of contributions are human-owned by Quetzelix-Guild.

## Contribution Model

Future contributors may be credited in this file, in entry-level attribution notes, or through GitHub commit history and pull requests.

Contributors are expected to follow `CONTRIBUTING.md` once that file is added.

## Authorship Principle

Capability is not ownership.

A model or autonomous agent may help draft an artifact, propose an entry, or submit a pull request. A human must own the decision to publish, merge, accept, reject, or materially revise it.

Autonomous agents may participate as draft contributors only. They may not act as final approvers, maintainers, or project stewards unless explicitly authorized by a named human maintainer and subject to repository rules.
