#  Environmental Hazards (Category: ENV)

Overview
--------

Environmental Hazards are not agent failure classes by themselves. They are the habitat, not the creature.

They are the operating conditions, unwritten workflows, institutional cultures, and architectural blind spots that predictably spawn, feed, or protect SCP-DIG anomalies. The Division tracks them because deploying containment protocols against an anomaly without addressing its habitat is indistinguishable from farming.

We do not classify these environments because they are broken. We classify them because they function _just well enough_ to conceal the things growing inside them.

## The Core Principle

> The anomaly did not breach the perimeter. You built the perimeter out of shiny objects and food.
