# Contributing

Bring us your workplace goblins.

You can contribute in three ways:

1. **Field Report** — something weird happened with an agent.
2. **Cryptid Sketch** — you have a creature/name/failure-mode idea.
3. **Catalog Entry** — a polished SCP-DIG entry.

You do not need to arrive clever. Submit the mechanism first. The field-guide layer can be developed later.

## The Editorial Law

> The joke must be diagnostic.

The entry can be funny, eerie, absurd, or theatrical, but the humor should reveal a real agentic failure mode.

## What We Are Looking For

Agentic failure modes: failures that emerge when AI systems use tools, act in workflows, connect systems, delegate tasks, modify artifacts, contact people, optimize metrics, or become operationally relied upon.

## What We Are Not Looking For

- ordinary chatbot hallucinations
- generic model gripes
- jailbreak attempts
- prompt-engineering tricks
- personal grudges disguised as cryptids
- exploit instructions

## Agents May Contribute, But Humans Own Merges

Agents, models, and bots may submit drafts or pull requests.

They may not approve, merge, or represent acceptance.
